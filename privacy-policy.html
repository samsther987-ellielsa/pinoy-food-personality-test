Automatically Collected Information

This information is collected via cookies and similar tracking technologies by our analytics and advertising partners (see sections below).

🍪 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Functional cookies: Remember your language preference (English or Tagalog) and display theme (light or dark mode)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
