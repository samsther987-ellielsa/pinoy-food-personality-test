What is Pinoy Food Personality Test?

Pinoy Food Personality Test is a fun, interactive quiz that connects your MBTI personality type to a Filipino dish that matches your soul. Based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator framework, we pair each of the 16 personality types with an iconic Filipino food — from bold Sisig to comforting Sinigang. Plus, discover your K-Pop and K-Drama character twin!

How It Works

1
Answer 16 Fun Questions Quick personality questions about your daily habits, preferences, and social style.

3

Frequently Asked Questions

This test is designed for entertainment and cultural celebration. While it uses the MBTI framework's four dimensions, it is not a clinical psychological assessment. Think of it as a fun way to explore personality through the lens of Filipino cuisine!

The test consists of 16 questions — 4 for each MBTI dimension. Questions are randomly selected from a pool of 48 for a fresh experience each time you take the test.

We feature 16 iconic Filipino dishes including Adobo, Sinigang, Lechon, Halo-Halo, Sisig, Kare-Kare, Lumpia, Champorado, Kapeng Barako, Bibingka, Pancit Canton, Bulalo, Taho, Balut, Turon, and Tinola — one for each MBTI type!

Your quiz answers are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to any server. Only your final MBTI result is saved locally on your device so you can revisit it later. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
