About Lechon

Lechon is a whole roasted pig, slowly cooked over charcoal for hours until the skin is perfectly crispy and the meat is impossibly tender and flavorful. It is the undisputed centerpiece of Filipino celebrations — fiestas, weddings, Christmas, and any event worth celebrating. Cebu is particularly famous for its Lechon, which the late Anthony Bourdain called "the best pig ever." The preparation is a community event: someone tends the fire, another bastes the pig, and everyone anticipates the first crack of that golden skin.

Understanding the ENTJ Personality

The ENTJ personality type — known as The Commander or The Executive — combines Extraversion, Intuition, Thinking, and Judging. ENTJs are bold, decisive leaders who see obstacles as challenges to be overcome through strategic thinking and willpower. They naturally take charge of any situation and inspire others to follow their vision. ENTJs are driven by achievement and efficiency, always looking for ways to improve systems and lead organizations to success. Their confidence and determination make them natural leaders who thrive when implementing plans and driving results.
