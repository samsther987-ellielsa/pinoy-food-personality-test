About Taho

Taho is a warm Filipino street food made of fresh soft silken tofu, arnibal (sweet caramelized brown sugar syrup), and sago (tapioca pearls). Sold by roaming vendors who carry two large aluminum buckets on a bamboo pole across their shoulders, Taho vendors are a beloved part of Filipino morning routines. The cry of "Tahoooo!" echoing through the neighborhood streets signals the start of a good day. Taho represents the simple sweetness of Filipino community life.

Understanding the ENFJ Personality

The ENFJ personality type — known as The Protagonist or The Teacher — combines Extraversion, Intuition, Feeling, and Judging. ENFJs are natural-born leaders driven by a genuine desire to help others reach their full potential. They are warm, empathetic, and charismatic, with an extraordinary ability to connect with people and inspire them. ENFJs have a unique talent for seeing the best in others and motivating them to believe in themselves. Their strong interpersonal skills and organizational ability make them effective leaders who lead with their hearts as much as their minds.
