About Halo-Halo

Halo-Halo (literally "mix-mix" in Tagalog) is a layered shaved ice dessert that combines sweet beans, jellies, coconut strips, jackfruit, ube (purple yam) ice cream, leche flan, and more — all mixed together into a glorious, colorful creation. With roots in the Japanese kakigori brought to the Philippines before World War II, Halo-Halo evolved into something uniquely Filipino, incorporating local ingredients and becoming a beloved summer staple. Every ingredient brings something different, yet together they create perfect harmony.

Understanding the ENFP Personality

The ENFP personality type — known as The Campaigner or The Champion — combines Extraversion, Intuition, Feeling, and Perceiving. ENFPs are enthusiastic, creative, and sociable free spirits who find joy in connecting with people and exploring new possibilities. Their infectious energy and genuine warmth draw people to them naturally. ENFPs see potential everywhere and in everyone, and their optimistic outlook inspires others to dream bigger. They are driven by their values and a desire to make a positive impact, approaching life with curiosity, spontaneity, and an irrepressible zest for adventure.
