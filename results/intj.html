About Kapeng Barako

Kapeng Barako is a strong, aromatic coffee variety grown in the Batangas and Cavite provinces of the Philippines. The name "barako" comes from the Tagalog word for a stud bull, reflecting the coffee's bold, powerful character. Liberica beans give Barako its distinctive intense flavor and full-bodied aroma that sets it apart from commercial coffee varieties. Historically, Barako was the coffee of choice in Philippine cafes before international chains arrived.

Understanding the INTJ Personality

The INTJ personality type — known as The Architect or The Mastermind — combines Introversion, Intuition, Thinking, and Judging. INTJs are strategic thinkers who approach the world with a rational, analytical lens. They are driven by an inner vision that they work methodically to bring to life. Making up only about 2% of the population, INTJs are one of the rarest personality types. Their combination of imagination and reliability allows them to take their ideas and make them happen. They are natural problem-solvers who see inefficiency as a challenge to overcome, and their standards for themselves and others are exceptionally high.
