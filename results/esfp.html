About Crispy Pata

Crispy Pata is a deep-fried pork leg that achieves a glorious contrast: shatteringly crispy golden skin on the outside and tender, succulent meat within. It's the showstopper dish at Filipino celebrations — fiestas, birthdays, and holiday gatherings are incomplete without it. The preparation requires boiling the pork leg until tender, then deep-frying until the skin blisters and crackles. Served with soy-vinegar dipping sauce, Crispy Pata is meant to be shared and celebrated.
